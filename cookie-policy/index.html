Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how arcanesvault.com uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your navigation, improve performance, and provide an optimized virtual experience powered by Virtual Runes (VR).

By continuing to browse or interact with the Website, you acknowledge and accept the use of cookies described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function smoothly, remember preferences, enhance security, and improve performance.

Cookies used by arcanesvault do not contain personal data and cannot identify you as an individual.

2. Why We Use Cookies

arcanesvault uses cookies to:

  • Ensure smooth navigation and consistent page loading
  • Remember your visual and accessibility settings
  • Optimize performance of rune-based visual effects
  • Measure anonymous engagement statistics
  • Improve overall experience inside the Vault

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Website to function properly. Without them, certain features may not work.

3.2 Performance & Analytical Cookies

Used to collect anonymous data about how users interact with the Website. This helps us refine navigation and improve VR-based UI systems.

3.3 Functionality Cookies

These remember preferences such as:

  • Language settings
  • Visual and display states
  • Previously seen banners or notifications

3.4 Preference Cookies

These help the Vault recall certain UI choices to deliver a consistent rune-themed atmosphere during each visit.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some analytics or performance tools may place cookies through our Website. These are controlled by trusted third-party providers and operate anonymously.

arcanesvault does not control third-party cookie policies. Users should review their respective privacy statements when interacting with external websites linked from arcanesvault.

5. How You Can Disable Cookies

You can disable cookies directly in your browser settings. However, doing so may reduce Website performance or limit access to advanced UI features.

Depending on your device or browser, you can disable, block, or delete cookies under:

  • Privacy settings
  • Security settings
  • Site permissions
